Your Expert Panel

Meet the Speakers

Four respected voices across dental business, law, and patient care — sharing what it really takes to grow a profitable practice without compromising on ethics.

Levi Barlavi
Founder, Polished Legal
Healthcare Business Attorney

Founder of Polished Legal and a healthcare business attorney advising dentists and physicians on practice ownership, transactions, and growth strategies. With 15+ years of experience, Levi has led over 1,000 healthcare deals — including practice purchases, sales, and partnerships. He combines legal insight with clear communication to help professionals make informed decisions through complex, high-stakes transitions. J.D., Georgetown Law; B.A., UCLA.

Angela Golden
Dental Advisor, CEPA

A Results-Driven Dental Advisor and Certified Exit Planning Advisor (CEPA) with 30+ years of executive experience in dental practice ownership, management, and strategic growth. Angela helps private practice owners increase profitability, strengthen leadership systems, and maximize enterprise value — guiding dentists through associate buy-ins, partnerships, and practice sales. Her advisory work transforms clinically driven practices into scalable businesses with strong EBITDA and long-term equity value.

Benjamin Atkins
Past President
Oral Health Foundation

Dr. Ben Atkins is the immediate past president of the Oral Health Foundation and a general dental practitioner who owned a group of dental practices in the North West of England. His core driver is patient education — captured in his practice mission: "for all our patients to understand their mouths, preventative regime and treatment." His practices also held NHS contracts caring for hard-to-reach communities within the NHS Dental Pilot.

Lora Banicevic
Founder, Corner 32 Dental
Calm Practice Consulting

Founder and principal dentist of Corner 32 Dental in Sydney, founder of Calm Practice Consulting, and author of The Emotionally Intelligent Clinician. Dr. Lora is known for building a patient-centred practice that proves high standards, strong culture, and commercial success can coexist. She advocates for ethical growth where trust is the greatest revenue driver — supporting higher retention, referrals, and long-term value without pressure-based dentistry.
