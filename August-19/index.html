Panelist 01

Lori William

Partner & Consultant · Strategix Dental Consulting

Lori brings more than 32 years in dental and orthodontics, including 20 years as Owner of Strategix Dental Consulting. She is a profound, energetic, and captivating speaker on the business of dentistry and orthodontics — focused on treatment coordinating, leadership, and practice growth. Panelist 02

Joelle Klovanish

Joelle Klovanish is the owner of Benchmarks Financial, where she helps dental practices, DSOs, and acquisition-focused organizations create the financial clarity needed for growth, transitions, and acquisitions. Joelle believes accounting should do more than produce reports — it should create clarity and help practice owners make confident decisions.
