### **Objective**

Once someone registers for a Thriving Dentist Expert Panel, we would like to give them the option to customize and share an **“I Am Attending”** post on LinkedIn or other social platforms.

Example Website: [https://dykemaconference.com/social-media-kit/](https://dykemaconference.com/social-media-kit/)

This can help us increase visibility, encourage peer-to-peer promotion, and bring in more registrations for each weekly panel.

### **Proposed Feature**

After a registrant completes the registration form, they should be taken to a confirmation/thank-you page where they can:

1. View a ready-made **“I Am Attending”** graphic/post. (Link Canva \- https://canva.link/q9akq691ah11v99)  
2. Customize basic details such as:  
   * Their name  
   * Practice/company name  
   * Optional profile photo  
3. Download the customized graphic.  
4. Copy a suggested social media caption.  
5. Share the registration link with their network.

### **Example Text for the Graphic**

**I Am Attending**  
 Thriving Dentist Expert Panel  
 \[Panel Topic\]  
 \[Date\] | \[Time\]  
 Join us for a free expert panel

### **Suggested Caption**

I’m attending the upcoming Thriving Dentist Expert Panel on **\[Panel Topic\]**.

Looking forward to an insightful discussion with dental professionals and industry experts.

Register here: \[Registration Link\]

### **Why This Would Be Helpful**

This feature can help:

* Encourage registrants to promote the panel voluntarily.  
* Create more visibility on LinkedIn and social media.  
* Make sharing easy and professional.  
* Support weekly panel registration growth.  
* Build more excitement before each event.

### **Request**

Could you please review whether this can be added to our current registration page flow?

Ideally, we would like a simple and easy-to-use version where the registrant can customize the “I Am Attending” post after registering and download/share it.
