DSO or Stay Independent? Dentists Who've Done Both Tell You the Truth

The DSO conversation is full of pitches and strong opinions, but very little honesty about what really happens after the ink dries. Dentists who've sold, stayed independent, or done both share the financial truths, lifestyle shifts, and decisions they wish they'd thought through sooner. If a DSO has reached out to you, don't miss this hour.
